Anfield was built in 1884, but got initially rented by Liverpools local Rivals, Everton FC. The very first game at Anfield took place on the 28th of September 1884. Everton faced Earlstown in the league. Everton had no problems and won 5-0. In 1891, Everton moved out of Anfield as they were not happy with the cost of rentong Anfield, and one year later newly-founded Liverpool moved in. Their first match at Anfield was a 7-1 win over Rotherham in the league. At the time it could hold a maximum capacity of 30,000 fans. Anfiled is famous for its European nights especially in the Champions League. Fans love watching Liverpool play in the champions league at Anfiled as the crowds are loud and excited for the game. Teams fear playing Liverpool at Anfield. Liverpool held the second longest run for unbeaten games at home, second from Chelsea.
